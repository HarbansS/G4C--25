FOREST FIGHTERS

A Teen Trying Something New!

PITCH

In forest fighters, you are a person who decides to take a little getaway road trip, your car breaks down and you wander into the woods, getting lost. You find yourself stumbling into a shop where you can earn items for survival in exchange for bringing back trash that polluted the once rich forest. Use your swiping abilities to grab as much trash as you can before you hit too many animals, disturbing the ecosystem. Trade all of your trash in so you can build your dream getaway home!

THEME - NURTURE YOURSELF WITH NATURE!

Forest Fighters introduces the theme of Nurturing yourself with nature through the narrative it tries to portray. Being stranded in the green, animal filled forest may be scary to someone who is used to living in civilization. This forces the player to come to terms with their situation and start to find a way to live alongside nature through cleaning the forest, reviving the once so rich ecosystem it was without pollution and building their true dream getaway. To be able to understand and love nature you will need to build yourself into it.

MY WHY

I decided to tackle this theme with my game's concept by wanting to point out a specific part of the theme along with another issue in the world. I realized to be able to nurture yourself with nature, you will need to make sure that there will be one for you and future generations. Pollution in the ecosystem has had a large impact on the wildlife and plants that call forests their home. Nurturing yourself with nature first starts with you!
